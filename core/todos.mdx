---
title: Todos
description: Per-workspace task tracking
---

Each workspace has a built-in todo list for tracking tasks, sub-items, and progress.

## Managing todos

- **Add** — Type a new todo and press Enter
- **Toggle** — Click the checkbox to mark complete/incomplete
- **Edit** — Click the text to edit inline
- **Delete** — Remove a todo from the list
- **Reorder** — Drag to reorder items

## Referencing in chat

Type `@todos` in the chat composer to inject your todo list into the conversation. The agent can see your tasks and work through them systematically.

## Summary

The todo panel shows a summary of total items, completed items, and whether all tasks are done.

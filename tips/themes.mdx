---

Fury comes with three built-in themes. Change themes in **Settings**.

## Available themes

### Blend (default)

A custom dark theme with blue accents. Designed for comfortable extended use.

### Midnight

A high-contrast dark theme with deeper blacks and brighter highlights.

### GitHub

A theme that matches GitHub's color scheme, familiar to developers who spend time on github.com.

## Changing themes

1. Open **Settings** (<Tooltip tip="Ctrl+, on Windows/Linux">**Cmd+,**</Tooltip>)
2. Under **Theme**, select your preferred option
3. The theme applies immediately
